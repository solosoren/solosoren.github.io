Scene Extension

Course Project, Dec 2019

We extend grayscale artistic images from WikiArt with 4:3 aspect ratio into 16:9 aspect ratio. To do this, we train a Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) with a U-Net style architecture for our Generator and a Discriminator modeled after VGG-16. We show our results with various loss schemes for our Discriminator.
